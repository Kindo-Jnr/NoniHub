About Us

Noni Hub is a tech and entrepreneurship driven organisation with a mandate to empower the youth with technological and entreprenuerial skills to enable them start their business and get emplyed.

This is driven from a passion to create wealth and jobs by providing the youth access to world class digital skills. Core to our mandate is to inspire the youth to use IT as a means to starting green business.

To deliver top notch IT and business development training and support the next generation of promissing Entrepreneus. We aspire to be a platform that nuttures green start-ups in the digital economy.
